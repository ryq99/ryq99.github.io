### Movie Lifecycle Revenue Forecasting for Digital Distribution

**Problem:** Forecast movie revenue across the theatrical and home-entertainment lifecycle to support financial planning and budgeting.

**Approach:** Built machine learning and deep learning models using lifecycle signals and historical performance to predict total and weekly transactions.

**Tech:** Python, ElasticNet, Random Forest, XGBoost, Stacking, LSTM, CNN, SHAP, Plotly Dash

**Impact:** Achieved <11% error on annual revenue forecasting and reduced movie-level MAPE by ~15% in production.

### Movie Revenue Forecasting for Streaming Content Distribution

**Problem:** Identify catalog titles with the highest revenue potential for streaming platform distribution.

**Approach:** Built an XGBoost forecasting model to estimate next-quarter monthly revenue and generate data-driven content programming recommendations.

**Tech:** Python, XGBoost, Feature Engineering, SHAP

**Impact:** Reduced prediction MAPE by ~20% compared with benchmark models and improved monthly content selection decisions.
